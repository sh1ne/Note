# Creating App Icons

The app needs two icon files:
- `icon-192.png` (192x192 pixels)
- `icon-512.png` (512x512 pixels)

## Quick Solution: Use an Online Icon Generator

1. Go to [Favicon.io](https://favicon.io/) or [RealFaviconGenerator](https://realfavicongenerator.net/)
2. Upload a simple note icon or use their text-to-icon feature
3. Enter "Note" or "üìù" as the text
4. Choose a background color (black or dark gray)
5. Download the icons
6. Rename and place them:
   - `icon-192.png` ‚Üí `public/icon-192.png`
   - `icon-512.png` ‚Üí `public/icon-512.png`

## Or Use a Simple Note Emoji

You can also just use a simple design:
- Black background
- White note icon (üìù) or "N" letter
- Square format
